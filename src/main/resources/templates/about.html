About

UGAStats was started by Techzilla, whose members are Logan Scheid, Ja'Von Holmes, and Isaiah McCoy. It was completed as a term project for CSCI4370 at UGA, and as a site for fans of UGA football like us.

After creating an account you are able to access our statistic pages for coaches, teams, and players. Each of these pages has tables with simple easy to understand layouts.
